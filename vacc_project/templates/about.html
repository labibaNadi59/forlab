About
ShishuShield.BD

ShishuShield.BD is a Child Vaccination Management System designed to help parents, hospitals, and administrators manage vaccination activities in a simple and digital way.

Our goal is to reduce missed vaccinations, provide timely reminders, and make appointment management easier for everyone.

Parents can manage child information, book appointments, receive reminders, and rate hospitals after vaccination. Hospitals can manage appointments efficiently through the system.
